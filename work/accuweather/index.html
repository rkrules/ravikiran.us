Android Growth

  • Led strategy and redesign of the AccuWeather Android app, scaling users from 1M to 35M and improving ratings from 3.0 to 4.4 across 500K+ reviews.
  • Achieved #1 search result for “weather” on Google Play; ranked #2 among 500+ weather applications.
  • Increased advertising revenue by 150% in one year, contributing approximately $10M in incremental annual revenue to the organization.
  • Generated $1M profit from a paid premium app model over three years.
  • Managed vendor relationships with Google, Amazon, BlackBerry, and other major platform partners.
  • Featured by Google, Amazon, Verizon, and BlackBerry as a showcase partner app.

Connected TV & Wearables

  • Launched AccuWeather on 8+ connected TV platforms: Samsung, LG, Toshiba, Sharp, Panasonic, Opera, Vtion, and Mstar.
  • Shipped AccuWeather for wearables, including the Sony Smartwatch — one of the early consumer weather experiences on a wrist device.
  • Managed the full product lifecycle for each platform: design, development, launch, and iteration.

Multi-Platform Expansion

  • Launched 25+ products over three years across Android, iOS, BlackBerry OS7 & BB10, Nokia, and Amazon.
  • AccuWeather on BlackBerry was one of the top revenue generators for the company and among the top-ranked weather apps on the platform.
  • Established analytics and usability research practices that improved product decision-making across the team.
  • Built and mentored the product management team through hiring and coaching.
