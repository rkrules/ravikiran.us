Baltimore , Labor Day Aug 31 - Sept 2

September 05, 2013
I extended the already long weekend by a day and thus we set out to Baltimore. We set out on a Saturday, and reached around afternoon. Did nothing on Saturday ! , just stayed back and watched movies etc. On Sunday, after a heavy complimentary breakfast we walked outside to watch the cars racing right next to our hotel. We got some views from the parking lot above, and we walked to Aquarium. In what would typically take 10 minutes, we spent more than 30 minutes to reach the aquarium because road was blocked and was being used for racing. Aquarium was heavily crowded (of course), but it was great. I think I like the Georgia aquarium better. We got a caricature drawn. The inner harbor place is really nice and reminded me of Singapore. We walked around there for a bit. On Monday, we went to Rocky Point Beach Park and finished the day with a dinner at a Indian restaurant. On Tuesday, we went back to Aquarium to witness dolphins' training and then we visited Sandy Point - finally a 'real beach', but I miss the beaches of India, especially high waves.
