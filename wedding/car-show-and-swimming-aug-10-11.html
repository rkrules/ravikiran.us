Car show and swimming - Aug 10, 11

August 12, 2013
We drove down to beautiful Clearfield for a classic car show. Wifey mistakenly thought for a moment that it was a 'car sale' - oh boy I would have loved to purchase some of those especially if sold at the price of the respective year ;) One of the bikes was very impressive - it had all the features of a car including GPS, CD changer, etc. The owners used it to drive around the country and camping. Cars from '28 were present, until cars of 2010. Their lid was open so that we could get an idea of how the engines and interior changed/improved over time. On Sunday, we hiked around Green Furnace State Park. And did little bit of swimming.
