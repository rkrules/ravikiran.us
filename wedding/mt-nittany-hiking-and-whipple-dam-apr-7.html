Mt Nittany Hiking and Whipple Dam - Apr 7

April 08, 2013
I am not a big fan of celebrating monthly anniversaries, but the weather in State College kept us from going out for this long and on this warm weekend we decided to try our hike again. Last time we went hiking (couple of weeks ago), the mountain was still covered in snow and we'd just taken few pictures and returned. This weekend, the hike was good. More than 8 kms, and close to 3 hours. We took couple of pics at the marked outlooks.  (I forgot to resume map after a short break and missed recording a stretch). It was very nice weather and we had a good time. While on the way back, stopped at Sheetz and then made a surprise/unplanned drive to Whipple Dam. All in all, a great weekend. That's how we welcome Spring :)
