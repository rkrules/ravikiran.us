Pennsylvania Grand Canyon - Apr 28

May 11, 2013
Wanted to visit Penn mini Grand Canyon from some time.. Once again, I lazily put that name in the Google Maps and followed the directions. At the end of it, I was in the middle of Pine Creek Gorge or somewhere, still long away from the actual outlook or hiking spot, which is in Wellsboro. Nevertheless the drive was filled with scenic beauty, and along side a river. We stopped at couple of trail beginnings (lot of biking trails all around) and took a lunch break next to a river. After a short time, lot of people were canoeing as well. Eventually, we made it to Wellsboro, but didn't have enough time for zoo or longer 'Turkey hike'. We did a small hike and enjoyed the view of the 'mini Grand' canyon. Slideshow:
