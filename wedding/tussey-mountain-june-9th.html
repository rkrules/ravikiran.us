Tussey Mountain June 9th

July 30, 2013
Long time ago I had gone on hiking Tussey Mountain with Manu - it was 'Spruce Gap' trail. I wanted to repeat it but never remembered the exact trail or the location and went on couple of Tussey mountain hikes on different trails (Shingletown). Finally, this weekend, we went on this trail. This is one of the toughest hiking trails I have done so far here, around this place. We went entire trail, and at the end it was completely exhausting. It was close to 3 hours and we were totally drained by the time we returned. ** On 8th we roamed around school, and explored Penn State Arboretum and garden near by. It was gorgeous.
