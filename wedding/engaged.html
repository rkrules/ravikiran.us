Engaged

December 26, 2012
Looks like I got engaged this morning ! I wasn't there physically, but was present in spirit. I woke up early morning to stay tuned to the developments and activities back in bride's house. It felt strange that all the arrangements and discussions being made by two families would not only involve me in the future, but I am 'one' of the two key people. But weddings (and even marriages) in India are more of a confluence of two families than of two people - and many events leading to this point have often given me a feeling how much of an after thought I am in the whole process. I was explaining all this to a colleague the other day, who simply joked - "it is just a matter of getting you prepared. This point on wards in life, you will be an afterthought to your wife's decisions". :O Ah, these marriage jokes. And on the topic, I was looking up marriage quotes - let me just say they induce more fear ! Padmashree gave me live updates - with soundbites, pictures and constant messaging to let me what was going on. So it isn't that I missed much. However, there will be one more official engagement ceremony, a day before wedding, in my physical presence :) It has started to sink in.
