## Subject

SeaWorld Parks & Entertainment Inc. modernized its 12 branded websites (SeaWorld, Busch Gardens, Aquatica, Sesame Place) through systematic A/B testing and data-driven optimization, focusing on improving mobile usability and conversion rates across digital ticketing and experience purchasing channels.

- A/B tested conversion improvements valued in the tens of millions of dollars
- 30% improvement in website feedback scores
- 50% reduction in bottom-of-funnel failure rate
- 5–10% increase in cross-sells added to cart (specific experiment)
- 3–4% increase in cart-to-checkout progression (specific experiment)
- 1% lift in overall conversion (specific experiment)
- 40% improvement in latency for key user actions
