What’s your TED talk going to be ?

April 17, 2014
Uncategorized

How can I live and work towards a goal ?

Well, this is going to be premature because I haven’t given a TED talk. But I have watched a lot, in fact some days it is my daily routine to watch TED talks.

I am not sure what attracted me to TED talks, or I don’t remember the first one I watched. But I was hooked. During my MBA, I realized all the videos that were being shown by Professors were already watched by me. It became quite known that I was the TED Talk guy in the class. Once when a Professor asked if anyone has seen that video, people turned around and pointed at me. Of course, it helped that I shared quite a many videos on my Facebook.

I have attended TEDx conference 4 times. Every time I have come back inspired, full of energy and motivation to do something better for the world. I have been inspired by the passion many speakers have exhibited. Whenever I feel down I reach out to a TED talk. Somehow, my challenges seem irrelevant and the opportunities seem limitless.

At the back of this all, deep inside me I have a wish to deliver a TED talk someday. I don’t know what it would be about — some data analysis, or a research or a book or something totally unknown. But wondering what my TED talk would be about motivates me to go about my day’s work.

It might, for you too. Ask yourself this question everyday, ‘What is your TED talk going to be ? And how your today’s work is going to contribute to it?’.
