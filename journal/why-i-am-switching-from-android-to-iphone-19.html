Why I am switching from Android to iPhone

September 24, 2015
Uncategorized

I have explained this to wife and couple of friends, but jotting it down here as Medium tells me to ‘tell a story’. There is probably a simple reason that I am switching to iPhone but as I think deeper, it is because of who I am. The reason has little, not nothing, to do with the phone’s features or company’s reputation, “cool” image but more to do with who I am as a person. 

My story goes back to the time I started tinkering with personal computers. Before the ‘branded’ computers — mostly ‘laptops’, were a norm among my peers, we only used assembled computers. I bought my own hard disk, sound card, mother board, RAM, graphics card and assembled to form my computer. It was good fun, but more importantly, it was the freedom we cherished to mix and match best products in each category and put together a computer. It was teenager’s way of building a ‘house’. As time progressed I got tired of doing it, and started buying branded laptops or bought assembled computers.

A similar story followed with Linux and Open Source revolution. When most of my peers were comfortable or were happily exploring different screensavers and wallpapers, couple of us were busy formatting hard-disks to install Linux.
