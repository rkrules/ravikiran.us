Ello

October 28, 2014
Business
I signed up for Ello the other day after requesting for invites on my Facebook. There was something about that !  But once I signed up, I wasn’t sure what to do - it didn’t seem like it was obvious, plus I had no network. Now, it’s not the first time that has happened - I was one of the early adopters for many other sites as well such as Twitter, Facebook, Jaiku and many others..and it takes a while for network to build up if that platform is successful.
I kept thinking though - what would be the business model for a social media. What upgrades would I be willing to pay for ? There are very few other business models on internet that have been as successful and as common as an ad-based model. 
Advertising no matter how people hate it, has given us, the consumers enormous free software and tools that wouldn’t have been possible otherwise. It is a shame that a ton of people don’t ‘get’ advertising business model, despite using the very same products every day.
