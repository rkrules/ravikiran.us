War of the worlds

July 21, 2005
Movie talk
Unclear CD blurred the intentions behind creation of this movie. Looked like a mix of ET and Id4, but lacked a story,flow. I feel, I can watch scenes in any order. Chaos in the city, run run run. The scene in the basement is particularly gripping. Technically, this might be very advanced compared to Id4 or Jurassic Park, considering the advancements in the equipments used and in the technology. But something else is required, isn't it ? Or did I do a mistake of watching on small screen ?
