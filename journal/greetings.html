Greetings. 

We, the authors of this blog, are experienced in Product Management and User Experience industry. More than the experience, we are passionate about these fields. As much as these practices are said to be “new”, there is a ton of research and practice in other areas that can be applied to these fields as well. 


At the same time what makes this field all the more challenging and exciting is that these are new technologies and devices that constantly redefine the way users are consuming the content. This poses unique opportunity and challenge to the user experience aspects of an application.


Similarly the changing landscape in the apps (millions now), the app store rules (most of them unknown), device fragmentation and avalanche of new devices make the app development, management and marketing a complex practice.


Infrequently, we will also discuss the news or research in these fields and what we think of them. 


The main goal of this blog is to educate the beginners and to learn from experienced practitioners. As such, we encourage interaction on this blog. Feel free to question our thoughts, and to provide counter examples where possible.


Thank you.
