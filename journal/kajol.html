Kajol

June 11, 2005
Movie talk
Endorsement: She is a very choosy girl. Her endorsements have been very few. I think she is the only among good leading ladies not to have endorsed Lux. Apart from the recent ad with hubby Ajay Devgan, her previous ad was for Pepsi 6 years ago. BlogPics/kads2.jpg Now she is chosen as brand ambassadress for Asmi. What is interesting is she is not just approached for the endorsement but voted. BlogPics/Kads.jpg
An intensive consumer survey revealed that Kajol inspires women across India.
Movie: BlogPics/Kads1.jpg But what is more interesting for me is that she is acting in a movie. Amir Khan ( whom I will forget if his movie does not come for another year…man it is already 4 years after DCH !) is opposite her. Given her ability to steal the show from biggest stars (remember the sob-sob K3G?!), it would be interesting to watch Amir with Kads. If she has agreed to do the movie, the script must have something for her (I am hoping this as her hubby is not involved in the project...remember I-don’t-find-adjective-to-describe Dil kya kare?!). High from the success of his previous venture (Hum Tum), Kunal kohli will be directing the movie which is set to go on floors in August. Can’t wait for the first day first show :D BlogPics/Kads3.jpg
