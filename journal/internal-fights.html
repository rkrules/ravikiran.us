Internal fights

December 13, 2005
News
There is a saying in Kannada if I try to remember means (close to), that the family quarrels should be kept within the closed doors. When we do not sort out our fights and blow it out of proportion, we are only going to be hit. As you can see here (Kannada newspaper link), the earlier events of Mr. Devegowda vs Mr.Narayana Murthy caused embarassing situation for PM Dr Manmohan Singh in Kuala Lampur. Whose loss will it be if investors turn away? Beware, everyone is being watched.
