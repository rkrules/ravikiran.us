what are Indians known for ?

July 24, 2011
General
We may have several answers to that question, depending upon our contributions and achievements. But people who search on Bing think Indians are smart, rude, cheap, ugly, smelly, arrogant On Google people think, in addition to the above, that Indians are afraid of dogs and are curious to know why they are skinny, vegetarian, good at spelling: Interesting that Google searchers think Indians are pretty but Bing searchers think Indians are ugly. I am particularly happy with the possible change in perception about Indians' wealth that they are rich. PS: Not sure how many of those refer to Native Americans or a certain baseball team.
