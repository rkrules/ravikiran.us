Chennai rains

October 27, 2005
Life
Looks like it is Chennai's turn now after the heavy rains at Bangalore. It has been raining here from yesterday afternoon. Heard that trains (Chennai-Bangalore) had been cancelled for the previous weekend. Lucky that I had not come home.But this weekend it is Diwali and planned since long. It looks dark even during the day. No power. Wish I was in school and it was holidays now!
