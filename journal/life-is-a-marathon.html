Life is a marathon

August 18, 2011
General Fun Videos
You may remember the Free Hugs campaign, which was a campaign to give hugs to strangers. It was banned by police and a petition removed the ban. It later became a youtube sensation, thanks to the music by Sick Puppies. Here is the video: Put the image.

Read more about it here.

Smile is precious and for some, rare. The joy of getting someone to smile is beyond the words. In an act similar to that of Free Hugs, volunteers stood in the subway station and cheered people who were returning from work, in a way marathon runners are cheered. See for yourself the reactions.
