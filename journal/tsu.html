Tsu

October 29, 2014
Business

Soon after Ello , came along Tsu -another social network, but the one promises to share revenue with the users. At least the interface is very familiar, easy to use and already has Android app (which is rare these days with startups and if I see Android app, it seems like startup has been through some validation and is more stable). 

But is ‘money’ enough motivator ? Other products have failed with only money as motivation. But Tsu combines both money and social validation that is a huge factor on Fb. 

I honestly think uprooting FB is near impossible atleast in the next few years, despite the product being extremely confusing and complicated to use by many in the world. Whatsapp won with the ease of use and does one thing really well - easy communication. FB has too many business units and will continue to stay strong. 

But social networking is more than a product, it is all about network. Orkut in India was upstaged by Facebook in just a couple of  years, so it is theoritically possible for FB to be won over by some other network, but just that it is highly unlikely.  

Yet I am curious to see what happens in this space, because honestly I can use some other network as long as my network of friends an family is there.
