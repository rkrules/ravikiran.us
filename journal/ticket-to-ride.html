Ticket to Ride

August 25, 2018
General
MBA - in between assignments , may be during the preparation for Mario/Nintendo presentation or during a Thanksgiving break, I don't recall accurately the time other than it was between '09 and '11 but the venue was Vanessa's place. Sean introduced me to Ticket to ride - I always remembered that game and that time fondly, however didn't get back to playing it till recently. I used to look for it in thrift stores, but wasn't really eager to buy and play - due to various other priorities and life events, my mind wasn't clear. After a job change, place change, and possibly resting for a while I decided to get back to playing games. For my wife's birthday in 2016, I ordered Ticket to Ride Europe edition. Just that weekend even before the Europe edition arrived in mail, miraculously, we found the American edition in Savers, another thrift store. My joy knew no bounds. Armed with two packs - my first game with wife wasn't as fun. She was so upset , that I thought she wouldn't play it again with me. However, I have my convincing powers. Soon after we found new friends in the area - Sandy and Spoo, who immediately hooked on to games. We played Ticket to ride and other games almost non-stop for 3 days during Thanksgiving. They also bought a India-Swiss edition. During this time, we also introduced the game to cousin Bhavana and Vikky, who were too hooked, and with whom we played non-stop during the next Thanksgiving break. Another fond memory was when we carried the game to Prof Sajay's home. I proposed the game but as other talk and activities took time, I was going to let it go. But both Sajay and Samar were interested to play and we played till 2 AM in the morning. Ticket to ride has given us so many fond memories (and some fights) and has been a gateway drug for my friends so that I can pull them into playing other games. But no other game so far has matched the interest of my friends to play. Sometimes I get bored as I want to play different games, but the fond memories of ticket to ride exceeds anything else, so I can't complain much.
