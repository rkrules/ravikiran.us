Coincidences, AB Jr , Shelved Fanah

August 05, 2005
Movie talk
Coincidences: I did not realise when Shabana Azmi won the national award for the movie that was not even released that year, that it was just a co-incidence. Later when Ajay Devgan won , it was again a co-incidence that the chairman of jury, Prakash Jha, had just directed Devgan. Now, Sharmila is the censor board director and time for one more co-incidence and surprise- Saif Ali Khan winning the best actor award. But somehow I can't stomach this co-incidence - The chairman of jury Sudhir Misra (did I know him earlier? It seems that Sudhir is a relative of a powerful politician), is promised a role in Yash Chopra's next venture, and Veer Zaara wins the award for best entertainment. Excuse me?! Here are my suggestion of some other departments 1) Poetic : Remember Main Khaidi number 786 ? 2) Makeup : Enough said 3) International Harmony or secular or whatever. 4) Editing Abhishek: Nobody seems to have noticed him when he gave a performance unexpected of a debutant in Refugee. Hrithik mania had overshadowed everything I guess. Now, after Yuva, Dhoom, Bunty and Sarkar, he is being taken seriously and enjoys a star status. Also all the pretty girls and heriones in town are chanting his name on every possible occassion - has it got to do with his "most-eligible-available-bachelor" status? Shelved Fanah: After a long time I bought a film magazine, jumping at the Kajol's pic on the cover of Stardust. Or I bought disappointment . It reports (link given below) that the keenly awaited movie of Kads with Aamir directed by Kunal Kohli, rumoured to be titled Fanah, is shelved. Reason? Ego clashes. Apparantely, unable to digest the equal footage given to Kads(talk of insecurities!!), Aamir restructured the script (or fractured it) and skimmed Kads' role. Having declined her best pals, if she had agreed to Kunal it was only because of the script. Take that itself away? She could have simply rejected it, but being the girl she is, went a step ahead. She redisigned the script and sent it back !! Now thats fun and tit-for-tat! While Kunal pulls his hairs, her fans would hope and wait for her much awaited comeback. To be noted that title means "ruined". And another coincidence to end the post: Today Kads celebrates her birthday.
