Free food =/= culture

October 14, 2014
Business

One of my favorite random questions to ask interviewers is to ask them about culture. I ask this same question to all interviewers and the answers I get are surprising. Many times they don’t match !

That aside, ‘free food’ , good work-life balance etc is not culture. Those are all perks and benefits. Culture is 'how’ you work and what values you carry. Sometimes it touches upon 'why’ you do what you do as well, most most of the time, the culture should answer 'how’ you work. 

There can be many answers to how - collaborative, fun, fast paced, aggressive, laid back, stressed, bottom up/top down, bureaucratic, and so on. I know some of the above can’t be said - what can’t be said is usually the answer too. For example if you are not able to say explicitly that your culture is relaxed, it is definitely not relaxed but towards stressed.

I remember in one of the session from DuPont, all the HR talked about was 'safety’ as a culture. Safety has nothing to do with what DuPont explicitly builds, but everything to do with how employees work in DuPont. 

When I hear 'free food’ as culture, I just wonder what’s beyond the curve.
